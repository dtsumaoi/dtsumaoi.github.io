ABOUT US

COMPANY ROOTS

We trace our beginnings to Nader Advertising –an advertising agency, which was founded in 1990 in the island-state of Bahrain. Its clients were:

  • Bahrain International Airport ⦾
  • Bahrain International Exhibition Center ⦾
  • Bahrain-Saudi Causeway ⦾
  • YK Commercial Center ⦾
  • Caltex ⦾

Due to bullish economic uptrend in the Philippines, in 1995, the founders decided to establish a signage manufacturing company in Metro Manila under the name of Bermon Global Ventures Corporation.

OUR WORK

Bermon Global Ventures Corporation is self-contained with our own group of exceptional creative staff, up-to-date equipment, advanced machines, and highly qualified machine operating professionals trained abroad with international standards in modern signage technology.

We offer top-of-the-line quality services that cater to the needs of our local and international clients – from delivery, installation, to the maintenance of materials and products.

PRODUCTS AND SERVICES

BGVC’S capabilities range from laser cutting services to the fabrication and
installation of 5 meter built-up channel letters 2 story pylons, to oxidized metal, sculptures.
